MVC Architecture Design:

1. Model: URLData
- long_url: str
- short_url: str
- custom_alias: str
- click_count: int
- expiration_date: datetime

2. View: URLShortenerView
- display_short_url(short_url: str)
- display_error(error_message: str)

3. Controller: URLShortenerController
- create_short_url(long_url: str, custom_alias: str)
- redirect_to_long_url(short_url: str)
- get_click_stats(short_url: str)
- delete_expired_urls()
