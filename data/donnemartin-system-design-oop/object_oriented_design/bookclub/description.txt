### **Virtual Book Club Platform**

**Overview**:
A platform that facilitates the creation and management of virtual book clubs, allowing users to discuss books, schedule meetings, and share recommendations in a community-driven environment.

**Functional Requirements to implement**:

1. **Book Club Creation and Management**:
   - [ ] 1.1. Users can create a new book club with a unique name and description.
   - [ ] 1.2. Club creators can set the club as private or public.
   - [ ] 1.3. Users can join existing public book clubs or request to join private clubs.
   - [ ] 1.4. Club administrators can manage member requests and permissions.

2. **Meeting Scheduling**:
   - [ ] 2.1. Integrated calendar for scheduling club meetings.
   - [ ] 2.2. Automated meeting reminders via email or in-app notifications.
   - [ ] 2.3. Option to integrate with external calendar apps (Google Calendar, etc.).

3. **Discussion Forums**:
   - [ ] 3.1. Forums for each book club for ongoing discussions.
   - [ ] 3.2. Threaded comments and replies in forums.
   - [ ] 3.3. Option for users to upload images or links to enhance discussions.

4. **Book Selection Process**:
   - [ ] 4.1. Club members can suggest books for reading.
   - [ ] 4.2. Voting system for selecting the club's next book.
   - [ ] 4.3. Integration with book databases for information and reviews.

5. **User Profiles**:
   - [ ] 5.1. Personalized profiles for users to share their reading interests.
   - [ ] 5.2. Users can list books they have read or wish to read.
   - [ ] 5.3. Follow system to connect with other readers.

6. **Recommendation Engine**:
   - [ ] 6.1. Automated book recommendations based on user's reading history.
   - [ ] 6.2. Highlight popular books within the community.

7. **Admin Dashboard**:
   - [ ] 7.1. Administrators can manage book clubs and user accounts.
   - [ ] 7.2. Ability to remove inappropriate content or users.
   - [ ] 7.3. Analytics on user engagement and popular books.

8. **Notifications and Alerts**:
   - [ ] 8.1. Customizable notifications for club activities and new discussions.
   - [ ] 8.2. Email alerts for upcoming meetings or important announcements.

9. **Resource Library**:
   - [ ] 9.1. A section for sharing reading resources, guides, and articles.
   - [ ] 9.2. Option for users to contribute to the resource library.
