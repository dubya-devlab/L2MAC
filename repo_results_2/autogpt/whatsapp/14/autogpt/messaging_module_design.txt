Messaging Module Design:
1. Text Messages:
   - Allow users to send and receive real-time text messages
   - Store the messages securely
2. Read Receipts:
   - Indicate when a message has been read by the recipient
3. End-to-End Encryption:
   - Encrypt messages to ensure they can only be read by the sender and the recipient
4. Image Sharing:
   - Allow users to share images in their messages
5. Emojis, GIFs, and Stickers:
   - Allow users to include emojis, GIFs, and stickers in their messages
