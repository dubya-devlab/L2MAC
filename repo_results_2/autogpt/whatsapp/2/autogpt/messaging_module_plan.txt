1. Sending and Receiving Messages:
- Users should be able to send and receive real-time text messages.
- The system should support end-to-end encryption to ensure the privacy of the messages.

2. Read Receipts:
- The system should support message read receipts, showing when a message has been read by the recipient.

3. Image Sharing, Emojis, GIFs, and Stickers:
- Users should be able to share images, emojis, GIFs, and stickers in their messages.
- The system should support these types of content in a secure and efficient manner.
